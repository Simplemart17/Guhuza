Welcome to Guhuza

Guhuza is a web-based game that engages users through the job-seeking and hiring process. Compete with other players, improve your skills, and experience the excitement of landing your dream job in a fun and interactive way!

How to Play

To start playing, click on the 'Play' button in the navigation bar. You'll be guided through various job-seeking scenarios and challenges. The goal is to apply, interview, and secure a job while competing with other players for the top score!

Benefits of Playing

  • Improve your job-seeking skills
  • Network with other players
  • Win exciting prizes and rewards
